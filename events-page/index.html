Cultural Fest of IIIT Guwahati

4 - 5 March, 2023.

About Yuvaan

One of the most awaited events in North East India is back with a bang to fill your hearts with fun and enthusiasm. Yuvaan is the annual cultural fest of IIIT Guwahati. This year, we have a vision to witness YUVAAN at new horizons, bring more hidden talents on display, award and appreciate every curious and skillful mind with a lot of thrill and amusement.Yuvaan is a celebration of art, culture, and youth for young and creative minds.
